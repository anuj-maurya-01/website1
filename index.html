Khwaab...

Mere chhote-chhote khwaab hain ki tum mere saath ho, Kahi baithe ho hum dono bas, Aur mere haathon mein tumhara haath ho, Main tumhein pyaar se dekhun, toh tum thoda sharma jana, Apne pyaar ko mujhe apni aankhon se batana, Kuch bole na hum dono, par phir bhi saari baat ho, Mere chhote-chhote khwaab hain ki tum mere saath ho.
"
— Anuj Maurya
"

Kya karein janab, hum pyaar jo unse karte hain...

Unke laakh mana karne ke baad bhi, hum phir bhi unpar marte hain, Kya karein janab, hum pyaar jo unse karte hain. Unke har diye dard ko hum hanste-hanste, nazar andaz karte hain, Kya karein janab, hum pyaar jo unse karte hain. Khud udaas hokar unhein manaaya karte hain, Kya karein janab, hum pyaar jo unse karte hain. Wo jab bhi bulaaye humein, hum daudke aaya karte hain, Kya karein janab, hum pyaar jo unse karte hain. Wo apni dosti humse nibhaate hain, hum pyaar nibhaaya karte hain, Kya karein janab, hum pyaar jo unse karte hain.
"
— Anuj Maurya
"

Kya rang hai ishq ka, log kehte hain ki laal ishq hai...

Kya rang hai ishq ka, log kehte hain ki laal ishq hai, Jo ek baar chadhkar utarta nahin, aisa bemisaal ishq hai, Kisi ko raja banata hai, kisi ko khaak mein milata hai, Yeh us par nirbhar karta hai ki kisko kitna chahta hai, Ishq mein maar khata hai ya saltanat jeet ke lata hai, Jo himmat bhar de logon mein, aisa yeh kamaal ishq hai, Kya rang ishq ka, log kehte hain ki laal ishq hai
"
— Anuj Maurya

About Lekh Verses

Lekh is a creative space dedicated to the art of verses, poetry, and shayari. It is built on the belief that words, when arranged with honesty and depth, can transcend boundaries and speak directly to the soul.

The writings featured here are a reflection of varied human emotions—love, longing, nostalgia, and introspection. Each piece is composed with a focus on clarity, rhythm, and emotional resonance, blending Hindi, Urdu, and English to create a voice that is both contemporary and timeless.

Through Lekh, the aim is not only to share poetry but to cultivate a space where readers can pause, reflect, and find fragments of their own stories within these lines. It is an invitation to experience literature that is intimate, evocative, and enduring.
