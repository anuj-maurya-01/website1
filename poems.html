Ajnabee

Husn ke hum unke kaatil ho jaate hain,
Jab wo roz subah apni zulfain lehraate hain,
Ab bhala koi kaise unse pyaar na kare,
Hum shaayar kaise unke husn ka deedar na karein.

Unke chehre ki muskaan kya hi kamaal dikhaati hai,

Roz humein unke deedar ko baahar bulaati hai,
Yahaan har koi unki adaaon ka maara hai,
Tabhi ye shaayar unpe apna dil haara hai.

U roz jaise unki aankhein kuchh sawaal kar jaati hain,

Ya phir yun kahoon ki wo dosti se ghabraati hain,
Haan ho sakta hai unhein thoda dar ho,
Yahaan ki na ho wo, kahan door unka ghar ho.

Kal chaand se unhone shayad pehli mulaqat ki,

Thodi der hi sahi par kisi se toh baat ki,
Kal unke chehre par pehli baar muskaan dekhi,
Jaise murjhaaye hue phool mein maine phir se jaan dekhi.

Kahaan na kahaan shayad humari bhi mulaqat hogi,

Kabhi na kabhi humari bhi unse baat hogi,
Tabhi sunana doonga ye shaayriyan unko kisi bahane se,
Hum aapke shaayaana aashiq hain, humein mat milana jamaane se.

Husn ke bazaar

Achha ishq hai, humse kya keemat lagaoge,
Yun abhi jitna chahte ho, kya waise hi reh paoge,
Rooh se mohabbat hai ya dil se chahoge,
Kahaan husn ke bazaar mein bik toh nahin jaoge.
Kabhi achhe lamhe honge, kabhi ladaiyan bhi hongi,
Gussa aayega mujhpar aur tanhaiyan bhi hongi,
Kya tab bhi mujhe tum yun hi manaoge,
Kahaan husn ke bazaar mein bik toh nahin jaoge.
Achha, chalo tumne humein pa liya,
Hum tumhare ho gaye, iska jashn bhi mana liya,
Kya us paane ki chahat ko zinda rakh paoge,
Kahaan husn ke bazaar mein bik toh nahin jaoge.
Jo waade tum kar rahe ho, usko toh nibhaoge,
Tumhare liye chhod ke aaungi sab, kahin bhool toh nahin jaoge,
Waise hi rahungi main jaisa tum chahoge,
Kahaan husn ke bazaar mein bik toh nahin jaoge.
Duniya ki baaton mein aakar kahin bhool toh nahin jaoge,
Manungi sach wahi jo tum bataoge,
Tum mere liye kaafi rahoge, kya mujhko bhi apne liye rakh paoge,
Kahaan husn ke bazaar mein bik toh nahin jaoge.
